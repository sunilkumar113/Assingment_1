Andrea Jeremia

 Andrea Jeremiah is a talented Indian actress, singer, and musician known for her work primarily in the Tamil film industry. With a background in classical music, she brings a unique depth to her vocal performances. Andrea's versatility shines through her diverse roles on screen, earning her critical acclaim and a strong fan following. In addition to acting, she has also made a mark as a playback singer, lending her voice to several hit songs. Known for her elegance and charm, Andrea continues to be a prominent figure in South Indian cinema.
